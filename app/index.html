RECRUITMENT AND ENGAGEMENT TOOL

Collect and manage your customers’ contacts. This tool can provide an easy way to automatically contact customers or employees: up-to-date emails or phone numbers, social network links, status messages, etc. You can add any personal information for easy organisation and communication.

discussion room

You can discuss any topic here, and participants can rank their discussions - like or dislike, comment, add their own thoughts or emotions. You can form a group of experts and compare discussions among different target audiences.

Questionnaire Tool

A tool for developing questionnaires with different type of questions, which you are able to edit. Your customers are then able to complete these questionnaires either on- or offline.
Ask the right questions. Gather your clients’ opinions and get answers in real-time instead of conducting slow, tedious classical research.

any-device

We keep you connected to your customers wherever they are by giving them the opportunity to use the devices that are most comfortable for them (PCs, tablets, mobiles).

real-time sms or email alerts

Immediately receive SMS or email alerts from disappointed customers and get a chance to solve the problem. Even if you don’t solve the problem, you can offer unhappy customers the opportunity to express their dissatisfaction immediately, you can also decrease the risk of customers leaving negative online reviews. Prevent negative feedback

automatic analysis tool

See your business through the eyes of your customers, partners and employees, 24 hours a day. Gain perspective on your business.

Role-based users. Reports are shown to each individual according to the roles they play inside your organisation. A TOP manager needs to control and act on tasks different to those of, for example, a secretary.

WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO

We believe in challenging the status quo and in real innovations that change companies and industries and the way we live our lives.

We believe that when people pay for something, they ought to be happy with it. And if they aren’t, then their concerns ought to be heard. Heard by someone from the company who has the power to help. We believe in win-win strategies: When companies help their customers, customers do the same for companies.

HOW WE DO IT

Gathering feedback should be fresh, simple and convenient for customers, and useful for companies. We want your customers to enjoy communicating with you and even do your marketing for you.

We challenge the status quo by helping you get to know your customers better faster so you can help them spread the word about your company that much quicker.

WHAT WE DO

That’s why we created Catch Feedback, user-friendly software that offers two-way communications: you won't just receive your customers' compliments and suggestions through our platform; you can also use it to respond to them and interact with them.
